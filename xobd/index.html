Diagnostics By Control Module

There are various control modules in a vehicle that are responsible for specific functions. The XOBD All Scanner tool will list all the relevant control modules that are available in the car.

Electronic Parking Brake Retraction

The brakes are released programmatically, allowing the maintenance works to be carried out.

Service Reset

The on-board computer tracks the maintenance mileage and maintenance cycles of the service. Both can be reset via the service reset option.

Steering Angle Sensor

The steering angle sensor can be reset after a wheel alignment or replacing a component ensuring the SAS system continues to function correctly.

Diesel Particulate Filter Reset

The DPF reset function allows the regeneration of the filter to take place by initiating the cleaning process to remove soot to ensure blockage free operation of the filter.

Throttle Body Position Reset

After certain maintence procedures such as disconnecting a battery cable, the throttle can be instructed to relearn to establish the correct idle speed.
