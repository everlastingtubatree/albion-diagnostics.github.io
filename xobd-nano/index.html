The scanner utilises a genuine M4 ARM processor, unlike other scanners sold on eBay, many of which utilise an illegal cloned copy of the ELM327 chip which utilises the M3 processor.

Key Highlights

Read DTC Codes

Easily perform a scan of engine related trouble codes and get detailed information from the in-built DTC library of codes regarding the fault without having to do a DTC lookup and an option to clear it.



Clear DTC Codes

Easily clear stored DTCs, DIYers can benefit by saving money on diagnostics checks and staying on top of issues before they begin to fester.

Dashboard

Provides fully customised dashboards on fuel consumption, RPM, torque, horsepower, coolant temperature, battery voltage, mass air flow, ignition timing and much more.

Heads Up Display

The dashboard has an option for it to be mirrored so the reflection can be seen in your windscreen, giving you your very own heads up display! The HUD option works best at night. WARNING: Please use this function responsibly and keep your attention on the road at all times. The scanner nor the app should be operated whilst your driving your car. Distractions could cause an accident resulting in death or injury.



Performance Testing

Want to figure out how long it takes for your car to go from 0-60mph. The performance testing feature allows you to calculate the time it takes for your car to accelerate and deaccelerate between two points.

Safety features

The scanner provides a set of safety alarms for speeding, fatigue and the coolant temperature, which are all user configurable. An effective approach to avoiding a speeding ticket!

Graphs

Get live detailed graphs showing RPM, torque and horse power to better understand the performance of your vehicle.

History

The history features allows you to go back and view historical data on troubles codes, live data, freeze frame and journey records.

Journey records

Journey records lets you view historical journey related data such as mileage, fuel consumed and duration of journey

Beware of illegal cloned copies of ELM327: Why should I buy this when I can buy a less expensive scanner?

There are cheap scanners you can certainly purchase, however many of these utilise an illegal cloned version of the original ELM327 chip. We do not fault the original ELM327 chip, which is comprehensive. HOWEVER, MANY ELM327 units sold on eBay are not genuine, they are cloned versions of the original, with incomplete OBDII support. Don't be fooled by some sellers claiming theirs uses a genuine chip. Our tool on the other hand, utilises a genuine M4 ARM processor with a FCC ID (certification can be provided on request) and with our own up to date proprietary software, allowing you to diagnose your vehicle safely, reliably and comprehensively. Please note there are a handful of scanners that do utilise the original ELM327 chip. See the Wikipedia page regarding cloned and illegal versions of the original ELM327 chip. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ELM327#Other_versions


Cloned and illegal versions of the ELM327 scanner come with either an illegal copy of a third party software or a trial version. You then have to purchase the pro version to unlock features. Our scanner uses our own proprietary software available on the app store and play store and updates are provide for FREE for LIFE!
